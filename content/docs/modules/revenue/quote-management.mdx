# Quote Management (CPQ)

Streamline your quoting process with Configure, Price, Quote (CPQ) capabilities. Ensure every quote is accurate, compliant, and profitable.

## Features

### Product Configuration
handle complex product logic and dependencies.
- **Bundles**: Group products together (e.g., "New Employee Laptop Kit").
- **Exclusion Rules**: Prevent selling incompatible items (e.g., "Wall Mount" requires "TV").
- **Attributes**: Select Size, Color, Capacity, etc.

### Pricing Engine
Advanced pricing logic beyond simple list prices.
- **Volume Discounts**: 10% off for 100+ units.
- **Contract Pricing**: Special negotiated rates for specific accounts.
- **Subscription Pricing**: Monthly vs. Annual billing options.

### Quote Generation
Create professional PDF proposals in one click.
- **Templates**: Branded headers, footers, and terms & conditions.
- **E-Signature**: Send directly to the customer for digital signature.

## Process Flow
1.  **Opportunity**: Sales rep identifies a deal.
2.  **Configure**: Rep selects products and options.
3.  **Price**: System calculates total based on rules.
4.  **Approvals**: Quote usually requires finance approval.
5.  **Proposal**: PDF sent to customer.
6.  **Order**: Converted to an Order upon signature.
