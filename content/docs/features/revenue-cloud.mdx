# Revenue Cloud (CPQ & Billing)

Stop managing quotes in spreadsheets. HotCRM **Revenue Cloud** brings complex product configuration and reliable billing directly into the CRM.

## CPQ (Configure, Price, Quote)

### 1. Product Configuration
*   **Bundles & Kits**: enforce logic like "Hardware A requires Warranty B" or "Product X is incompatible with Product Y."
*   **Guided Selling**: A wizard-like interface asking needs-based questions to recommend the right SKU mix.

### 2. Advanced Pricing Engine
*   **Multi-Currency Price Books**: Manage global pricing strategies.
*   **Volume Pricing**: Tiered discounts based on quantity (e.g., 1-10 units @ $100, 11-50 @ $90).
*   **Contract Pricing**: Customer-specific pre-negotiated prices.

### 3. Quote Generation
*   **One-Click PDF**: Generate professional proposals with dynamic terms and conditions.
*   **E-Signature**: Integrated signature collection to close deals faster.

## Subscription Management

*   **Asset Lifecycle**: Track what the customer owns/subscribes to over time.
*   **Amendments & Renewals**: Handle "Mid-term Upgrades," "Downgrades," and smooth "Renewal" quoting automation.
*   **ARR/MRR Tracking**: Real-time visibility into Annual Recurring Revenue and revenue churn.

## Billing & Finance
*   **Invoice Generation**: Automatically convert Closed Won Opportunities/Orders into Invoices.
*   **Payment Gateway**: Integration with Stripe/PayPal to collect payments.
*   **Revenue Recognition**: ASC 606 / IFRS 15 compliant revenue schedules.
