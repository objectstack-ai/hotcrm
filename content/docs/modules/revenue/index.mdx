# Revenue Cloud

The **Revenue Cloud** handles the complex financial lifecycle of your customers, bridging the gap between Sales and Finance.

## Functional Capabilities

### 1. CPQ (Configure, Price, Quote)
Generate accurate quotes for complex products.
- **Product Bundle Configuration**: Rules for valid product combinations.
- **Price Rules**: Volume discounts, partner pricing, and tiered pricing.
- **Quote Generation**: One-click PDF creation.

### 2. Contract Management
Manage subscriptions, renewals, and legal agreements.
- **Auto-Renewal**: Automatically generate renewal opportunities.
- **Amendments**: Handle mid-term upgrades/downgrades.

### 3. Billing & Invoicing
- **Invoice Generation**: Create invoices from closed orders.
- **Payment Tracking**: Record status of payments.

## Integration Points

- **Sales Cloud**: Converts Opportunities to Quotes.
- **Service Cloud**: Validates support entitlements.
