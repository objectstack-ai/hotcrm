# Sales Cloud

The HotCRM Sales Cloud provides everything you need to manage your sales process from initial lead capture through deal closure and beyond. With AI-powered insights at every step, your sales team can work smarter and close more deals.

## Core Capabilities

### Lead Management

Capture, qualify, and convert leads with intelligent automation.

**Key Features:**
- **Multi-Channel Lead Capture**: Web forms, landing pages, manual entry, imports
- **AI Lead Scoring**: Automatically score leads 0-100 based on fit and behavior
- **Lead Assignment**: Automatic routing based on territory, product, or criteria
- **Public Lead Pool**: Unclaimed leads accessible to all sales reps
- **Data Completeness Tracking**: Visual indicators of missing information
- **Duplicate Detection**: Prevent duplicate leads from entering the system
- **Lead Conversion**: One-click conversion to Account, Contact, and Opportunity

**Lead Lifecycle:**
1. **New** - Lead just entered the system
2. **Working** - Sales rep actively engaging
3. **Nurturing** - Not ready to buy, in marketing nurture
4. **Qualified** - Meets BANT criteria, ready for conversion
5. **Unqualified** - Doesn't meet criteria
6. **Converted** - Successfully converted to opportunity

**AI Intelligence:**
- Automatic lead scoring based on demographic and firmographic data
- Email signature parsing to enrich lead data
- Smart recommendations for next best actions
- Predictive conversion probability

### Account Management

Get a complete 360-degree view of your customers and prospects.

**Key Features:**
- **Company Information**: Name, industry, revenue, employee count, website
- **Account Hierarchy**: Parent-child relationships for complex organizations
- **Account Health Scoring**: AI-powered health metrics
- **Relationship Tracking**: Complete history of all interactions
- **Account Teams**: Assign multiple team members to strategic accounts
- **Territory Management**: Organize accounts by geography or other criteria
- **Customer Lifecycle Stages**: Prospect → Customer → Partner tracking

**Account Types:**
- **Prospect**: Not yet a customer
- **Customer**: Active paying customer
- **Partner**: Channel or strategic partner
- **Competitor**: Competitive intelligence tracking
- **Former Customer**: Win-back opportunities

**SLA Tiers:**
- **Platinum**: Premium support and response times
- **Gold**: Enhanced support level
- **Silver**: Standard support
- **Bronze**: Basic support
- **None**: No SLA commitment

**AI Intelligence:**
- Account health scoring and churn risk prediction
- Cross-sell and upsell opportunity identification
- Recommended next actions based on account activity
- Competitive intelligence analysis

### Contact Management

Build and maintain relationships with key decision-makers.

**Key Features:**
- **Contact Information**: Full profile with role, department, social profiles
- **Decision Chain Mapping**: Identify decision-makers and influencers
- **Influence Level Tracking**: Key Decision Maker, Influencer, End User
- **Relationship Strength**: Track relationship depth and quality
- **Contact Roles**: Map contacts to opportunities
- **Communication History**: All calls, emails, meetings in one timeline
- **Business Card Scanning**: OCR extraction from business cards
- **Social Integration**: LinkedIn, Twitter, WeChat profiles

**Decision Maker Types:**
- **Key Decision Maker**: Final authority on purchase
- **Influencer**: Influences the decision
- **Technical Evaluator**: Evaluates technical fit
- **Champion**: Internal advocate for your solution
- **End User**: Will use the product/service
- **Blocker**: May oppose the purchase

**AI Intelligence:**
- Contact data enrichment from email signatures
- Buying intent prediction
- Sentiment analysis from communications
- Optimal contact time recommendations

### Opportunity Management

Manage your sales pipeline and forecast revenue accurately.

**Key Features:**
- **7-Stage Pipeline**: Prospecting → Qualification → Needs Analysis → Proposal → Negotiation → Closed Won/Lost
- **Revenue Forecasting**: Predict revenue by stage probability
- **Win/Loss Analysis**: Track reasons and competitive intelligence
- **Deal Coaching**: AI-powered next steps and talking points
- **Collaborative Selling**: Team selling with shared notes and tasks
- **Document Management**: Proposals, contracts, presentations
- **Competitor Tracking**: Track competitors and win strategies
- **Risk Assessment**: Identify and mitigate deal risks

**Opportunity Stages:**

1. **Prospecting (10% probability)**
   - Initial contact made
   - Basic qualification underway
   - Exploring if there's a fit

2. **Qualification (20% probability)**
   - BANT criteria assessed (Budget, Authority, Need, Timeline)
   - Confirmed there's a genuine opportunity
   - Decision process understood

3. **Needs Analysis (40% probability)**
   - Requirements gathering complete
   - Solution being shaped
   - Key stakeholders identified

4. **Proposal (60% probability)**
   - Formal proposal submitted
   - Pricing presented
   - ROI demonstrated

5. **Negotiation (80% probability)**
   - Contract terms being discussed
   - Legal review in progress
   - Final pricing negotiations

6. **Closed Won (100% probability)**
   - Deal won! Contract signed
   - Customer onboarding begins
   - Success metrics established

7. **Closed Lost (0% probability)**
   - Deal lost to competitor or no decision
   - Loss reason captured
   - Opportunity for future engagement

**AI Intelligence:**
- **Win Probability**: ML-based prediction beyond stage probability
- **Risk Factor Detection**: Identify potential deal blockers
- **Next Step Recommendations**: AI-suggested actions to advance the deal
- **Talking Points**: Industry-specific conversation starters
- **Competitive Analysis**: Insights on how to beat competitors
- **Deal Acceleration**: Identify what will move the deal forward faster

## Sales Process Workflows

### Lead-to-Opportunity Conversion

The standard process for converting qualified leads:

1. **Lead Generation**
   - Lead captured from web form, event, or referral
   - Automatic lead scoring assigned
   - Assigned to sales rep based on territory

2. **Lead Qualification**
   - Sales rep contacts lead
   - BANT criteria evaluated (Budget, Authority, Need, Timeline)
   - Lead status updated to "Qualified"

3. **Lead Conversion**
   - Click "Convert Lead" button
   - System creates:
     - **Account** (if new company)
     - **Contact** (from lead information)
     - **Opportunity** (new sales opportunity)
   - All lead activities transferred to new records
   - Lead status set to "Converted"

4. **Opportunity Management**
   - Work the opportunity through pipeline stages
   - Log activities and update progress
   - Collaborate with team members
   - Close as Won or Lost

### Opportunity-to-Contract Workflow

When a deal closes:

1. **Opportunity Closed Won**
   - Opportunity stage updated to "Closed Won"
   - Win reason and amount captured

2. **Contract Generation**
   - Automatic contract record created
   - Contract terms from opportunity
   - Linked to account and opportunity

3. **Quote to Invoice**
   - Accepted quote converted to invoice
   - Payment terms established
   - Payment tracking begins

4. **Customer Onboarding**
   - Account status updated to "Active Customer"
   - Success metrics established
   - Customer success team engaged

## Best Practices

### Lead Management Best Practices

1. **Respond Quickly**: Contact new leads within 5 minutes for best results
2. **Score Consistently**: Use AI scoring but validate and refine regularly
3. **Nurture Wisely**: Not ready now doesn't mean not ready ever
4. **Clean Data**: Keep lead information current and complete
5. **Follow Up**: Persistence pays - plan multiple touchpoints

### Account Management Best Practices

1. **Regular Updates**: Keep account information current
2. **Map Relationships**: Identify all key stakeholders
3. **Track Health**: Monitor account health proactively
4. **Plan Strategically**: Create account plans for key customers
5. **Collaborate**: Share insights across the account team

### Opportunity Best Practices

1. **Qualify Early**: Don't chase bad opportunities
2. **Set Next Steps**: Every opportunity should have a clear next action
3. **Update Regularly**: Keep stage and close date current
4. **Manage Risks**: Identify and address risks early
5. **Document Everything**: Capture competitive intel and requirements
6. **Collaborate**: Bring in specialists and executives when needed

### Activity Tracking Best Practices

1. **Log Everything**: If it's not in the CRM, it didn't happen
2. **Be Specific**: Detailed notes help the whole team
3. **Follow Up Promptly**: Schedule next activity before ending current one
4. **Use Call Scripts**: Consistency improves results
5. **Review Calls**: Listen to recordings to improve technique
