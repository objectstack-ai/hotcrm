# Sales Cloud

The **Sales Cloud** module is designed to help sales teams sell faster and smarter. It centralizes customer information, automates interactions, and provides AI-powered intelligence to drive deal closure. With 13 comprehensive objects in the CRM package, it delivers enterprise-grade sales automation.

## Functional Capabilities

### 1. Lead Management

Capture and qualify leads from multiple channels with AI-powered scoring.

- **Multi-Source Capture**: Web-to-Lead, Email, Manual Entry, API Import
- **Lead Scoring**: AI-powered scoring (0-100) based on demographics, behavior, and engagement
- **Duplicate Detection**: Automatic detection and merging of duplicate leads
- **Lead Assignment**: Automatic routing based on territory, industry, or lead source
- **Public Pool**: Shared lead pool for unclaimed leads
- **Lead Conversion**: Convert qualified leads to Account, Contact, and Opportunity
- **Data Completeness**: Automatic calculation of profile completeness percentage

**Objects**: `lead`, `assignment_rule`

### 2. Account & Contact Management

Build a complete customer 360 view with hierarchical relationships.

- **Account Hierarchy**: Parent-child company relationships for enterprise accounts
- **Industry Classification**: Standard industry codes with custom taxonomies
- **Revenue Tracking**: Annual revenue and employee count for prioritization
- **Contact Roles**: Decision makers, influencers, champions, and blockers
- **Social Integration**: Link to LinkedIn, Twitter, and other social profiles
- **Interaction Timeline**: Complete history of emails, calls, meetings, and notes
- **Account Teams**: Assign multiple team members to strategic accounts

**Objects**: `account`, `contact`

### 3. Opportunity Management

Manage the deal lifecycle with visual pipeline and AI insights.

- **7-Stage Pipeline**: Prospecting → Qualification → Needs Analysis → Proposal → Negotiation → Closed Won/Lost
- **Kanban Board**: Drag-and-drop deals across stages for visual pipeline management
- **Sales Path**: Step-by-step guidance for each sales stage
- **AI Win Probability**: ML-predicted likelihood to close based on deal velocity and engagement
- **Competitive Intelligence**: Track competitors and win/loss reasons
- **Deal Health Scoring**: Risk factor identification and next-step recommendations
- **Revenue Forecasting**: Roll-up forecasting from rep to VP level

**Objects**: `opportunity`

### 4. Activity Tracking

Log every customer interaction with AI-powered insights.

- **Activity Types**: Calls, Emails, Meetings, Tasks, Check-ins
- **AI Transcription**: Voice-to-text for call recordings
- **Action Item Extraction**: Automatic to-do creation from meeting notes
- **Sentiment Analysis**: Detect customer sentiment in communications
- **GPS Check-in**: Location tracking for field sales visits
- **Email Integration**: Sync with Gmail, Outlook for automatic activity logging
- **Timeline View**: Chronological activity feed by account or opportunity

**Objects**: `activity`, `task`, `note`

### 5. Marketing Integration

Seamless handoff from marketing to sales.

- **Marketing Lists**: Targeted lists for campaigns and outreach
- **Email Templates**: Personalized email templates with merge fields
- **Landing Pages**: Create conversion-optimized landing pages
- **Form Builders**: Embed lead capture forms on websites
- **Campaign Attribution**: Track which marketing campaigns generate revenue
- **Lead Source Tracking**: Multi-touch attribution from first touch to close

**Objects**: `marketing_list`, `email_template`, `landing_page`, `form`

### 6. Email Compliance

Stay compliant with anti-spam regulations.

- **Unsubscribe Management**: One-click unsubscribe with preference center
- **Consent Tracking**: GDPR/CCPA compliance with audit trails
- **Email Blacklist**: Automatic suppression of unsubscribed contacts

**Objects**: `unsubscribe`

## AI-Powered Features

Intelligent automation throughout the sales cycle:

- **Lead Scoring 2.0**: Behavioral + demographic + intent signals
- **Email Enrichment**: Extract company info from email signatures
- **Next Best Action**: AI suggests optimal next step for each deal
- **Win Probability**: Predict likelihood to close with confidence intervals
- **Talking Points**: AI-generated industry-specific sales suggestions
- **Email Drafting**: AI-composed personalized outreach emails
- **Meeting Prep**: Auto-generated briefings before customer meetings
- **Churn Prediction**: Identify at-risk customers before renewal

## Integration Points

- **Marketing Cloud**: Receives qualified leads (MQL) from campaigns
- **Revenue Cloud**: Sends closed deals for quoting and billing
- **Service Cloud**: Links support cases to accounts for complete customer view
- **HR Cloud**: Maps employees to customer accounts for relationship tracking

## Sales Metrics

Track sales performance with real-time dashboards:

- **Pipeline Value**: Total value of open opportunities
- **Win Rate**: Percentage of opportunities closed-won
- **Average Deal Size**: Mean opportunity amount
- **Sales Cycle Length**: Average days from create to close
- **Lead Conversion Rate**: Percentage of leads converted to opportunities
- **Quota Attainment**: Individual and team progress against targets
- **Forecast Accuracy**: Predicted vs. actual closed revenue
- **Activity Metrics**: Calls, emails, meetings per rep
