# Revenue Cloud

The **Revenue Cloud** handles the complete financial lifecycle of your customers, bridging the gap between Sales and Finance. It combines product catalog, pricing rules, quoting (CPQ), contract management, invoicing, and payment tracking into a unified revenue engine.

## Functional Capabilities

### 1. CPQ (Configure, Price, Quote)

Generate accurate quotes for complex products with intelligent pricing.

- **Product Bundle Configuration**: Define valid product combinations with constraints
- **Price Rules**: Volume discounts, partner pricing, tiered pricing, and promotional pricing
- **Multi-Currency Support**: Quote in 8 currencies (CNY, USD, EUR, GBP, JPY, HKD, SGD, AUD)
- **Discount Management**: Tiered approval workflows for discounts (0-10%, 10-20%, >20%)
- **Quote Generation**: One-click PDF creation with customizable templates
- **Product Recommendations**: AI-suggested product bundles based on customer needs

**Objects**: `product`, `product_bundle`, `product_bundle_component`, `quote`, `quote_line_item`, `pricebook`, `price_rule`, `discount_schedule`, `approval_request`

### 2. Contract Management

Manage the entire contract lifecycle from creation to renewal.

- **Contract Creation**: Auto-generate contracts from closed opportunities
- **Amendment Tracking**: Handle mid-term upgrades, downgrades, and modifications
- **Auto-Renewal**: Automatically generate renewal opportunities 90 days before expiration
- **E-Signature Integration**: Ready for DocuSign/Adobe Sign integration
- **Version Control**: Track contract amendments with full audit trail
- **Expiry Alerts**: Automated notifications for upcoming renewals

**Objects**: `contract`

### 3. Billing & Invoicing

Streamline invoice generation and payment collection.

- **Invoice Generation**: Create invoices from contracts with line-item detail
- **Payment Plans**: Support for installment payments and milestones
- **Payment Tracking**: Record payment status (Pending, Partial, Paid, Overdue)
- **Automated Reminders**: Email alerts for upcoming and overdue payments
- **Multi-Currency Invoicing**: Support for international transactions
- **Tax Calculation**: Automatic tax computation based on jurisdiction

**Objects**: `invoice`, `invoice_line`, `payment`

### 4. Product Catalog Management

Centralized product information for sales and marketing.

- **SKU Management**: Product codes with variants and families
- **Product Families**: Group related products for easier management
- **Inventory Tracking**: Stock levels with low-stock alerts
- **Multi-Unit Support**: Sell by unit, case, pallet, or custom UOM
- **Product Lifecycle**: Active, Inactive, End of Life status tracking
- **Sales Collateral**: Attach datasheets, images, and product videos

**Objects**: `product`, `product_bundle`

### 5. Pricing Strategy

Flexible pricing to support any business model.

- **Tiered Pricing**: Volume-based price breaks (e.g., 1-10 units: $100, 11-50: $90)
- **Regional Pricing**: Different prices by geographic region
- **Channel Pricing**: Separate pricing for direct, partner, and online channels
- **Promotional Pricing**: Time-limited special offers
- **Customer-Specific Pricing**: Contract pricing for enterprise accounts
- **Cost-Plus Pricing**: Automatic margin calculations

**Objects**: `pricebook`, `price_rule`, `discount_schedule`

## AI-Powered Features

Intelligent automation throughout the revenue cycle:

- **Smart Product Bundles**: AI recommends optimal product combinations based on customer budget
- **Win Probability**: Predict quote acceptance rate before sending
- **Optimal Pricing**: AI suggests competitive pricing based on market data
- **Discount Optimization**: Recommend maximum discount while maintaining target margins
- **Renewal Prediction**: Identify at-risk renewals for proactive retention
- **Upsell Opportunities**: Detect expansion opportunities in existing accounts

## Integration Points

- **Sales Cloud**: Converts Opportunities to Quotes and Contracts
- **Service Cloud**: Validates support entitlements based on contract status
- **Finance Systems**: Export invoices to ERP systems (SAP, NetSuite, QuickBooks)
- **Payment Gateways**: Process payments via Stripe, PayPal, or bank transfers

## Business Metrics

Track revenue health with real-time metrics:

- **Total Contract Value (TCV)**: Sum of all active contracts
- **Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR)**: Subscription revenue normalized to annual
- **Quote-to-Close Ratio**: Percentage of quotes that convert to contracts
- **Average Deal Size**: Mean contract value
- **Renewal Rate**: Percentage of contracts that renew
- **Days Sales Outstanding (DSO)**: Average time to collect payment
