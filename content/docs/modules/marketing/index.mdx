# Marketing Cloud

The **Marketing Cloud** drives demand generation through multi-channel campaigns and intelligent lead nurturing. Built as a modular plugin, it integrates seamlessly with Sales Cloud for end-to-end lead-to-cash workflows.

## Functional Capabilities

### 1. Campaign Management

Plan, execute, and track marketing initiatives across all channels.

- **Multi-Channel Support**: Email, Social Media, Events, Webinars, Paid Ads, Content Marketing
- **Budget Tracking**: Monitor "Actual Cost" vs "Budgeted Cost" with real-time ROI
- **Campaign Hierarchy**: Parent/Child campaign relationships for complex initiatives
- **Campaign Member Management**: Track individual engagement and conversion
- **Status Tracking**: From Planning → In Progress → Completed with success metrics

**Objects**: `campaign`, `campaign_member`

### 2. Lead Nurturing & Engagement

- **Drip Campaigns**: Automated email sequences triggered by behavioral signals
- **Behavioral Scoring**: Score leads based on website visits, email clicks, and content downloads
- **Marketing Lists**: Segmentation for targeted campaigns
- **A/B Testing**: Test messaging, offers, and creative variations
- **Email Templates**: Reusable email templates with personalization tokens

**Objects**: `marketing_list`, `email_template`

### 3. Lead Generation

Capture leads from multiple digital channels.

- **Landing Pages**: Create conversion-optimized landing pages with form builders
- **Web Forms**: Embed forms on your website with spam protection
- **Social Lead Ads**: Integrate with LinkedIn, Facebook lead generation
- **Event Registration**: Capture leads from webinars and conferences

**Objects**: `landing_page`, `form`

### 4. ROI Analytics & Reporting

Measure marketing effectiveness with precision.

- **Attribution Analysis**: Link closed revenue back to the originating campaign
- **Cost Analysis**: Calculate Cost Per Lead (CPL), Cost Per Acquisition (CPA), and ROI
- **Funnel Metrics**: Track conversion rates from Lead → MQL → SQL → Opportunity → Closed Won
- **Channel Performance**: Compare effectiveness across marketing channels
- **Campaign Dashboard**: Real-time metrics on active campaigns

### 5. Compliance & Consent Management

Stay compliant with data protection regulations.

- **Unsubscribe Management**: One-click unsubscribe with preference center
- **Consent Tracking**: GDPR and CCPA compliance with audit trails
- **Data Privacy**: Automatic PII handling and data retention policies

**Objects**: `unsubscribe`

## AI-Powered Features

Every marketing process is enhanced with intelligent automation:

- **Content Generation**: AI-generated email copy, subject lines, and ad headlines
- **Audience Segmentation**: ML-powered customer clustering for targeted campaigns
- **Send Time Optimization**: Predict the best time to send emails for each recipient
- **Channel Recommendations**: AI suggests the best channels for each segment
- **Subject Line Scoring**: Predict email open rates before sending
- **Campaign Performance Prediction**: Forecast campaign ROI before launch

## Integration Points

- **Sales Cloud**: Hands off "Marketing Qualified Leads" (MQL) to sales teams
- **CRM Cloud**: Links campaigns to accounts, contacts, and opportunities for full attribution
- **AI Cloud**: Leverages AI models for content generation and predictive analytics
