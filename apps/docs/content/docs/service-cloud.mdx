# Service & Support Cloud

The HotCRM Service & Support Cloud delivers exceptional customer service with omnichannel case management, AI-powered ticket routing, SLA tracking, and a comprehensive knowledge base. Manage support tickets from any channel while ensuring fast, accurate resolutions.

## Core Capabilities

### Case Management

Comprehensive support ticket management from creation to resolution.

**Key Features:**
- **Omnichannel Intake**: Email, web, phone, social media, chat, mobile app
- **Case Routing**: Intelligent assignment to best agent
- **Priority Management**: Automatic priority assignment
- **SLA Tracking**: Service level agreement monitoring
- **Escalation**: Automatic escalation for breached SLAs
- **Customer Satisfaction**: Post-resolution surveys
- **Case History**: Complete interaction timeline
- **Collaboration**: Internal notes and @mentions
- **Knowledge Integration**: Suggested articles for resolution

**Case Channels:**
- **Email**: Email-to-case automatic creation
- **Web**: Web form submission
- **Phone**: Call center ticket creation
- **WeChat**: WeChat messaging integration
- **Chat**: Live chat widget
- **Mobile App**: In-app support
- **Social Media**: Twitter, Facebook, Instagram
- **Walk-In**: In-person support

**Case Status:**
1. **New**: Just created, not yet assigned
2. **Assigned**: Assigned to agent or queue
3. **Working**: Agent actively working on case
4. **Pending Customer**: Waiting for customer response
5. **Pending Internal**: Waiting for internal resource
6. **Escalated**: Escalated to higher tier
7. **Resolved**: Solution provided to customer
8. **Closed**: Customer confirmed resolution

**Case Priority:**
- **Critical**: System down, business stopped
- **High**: Major functionality impaired
- **Medium**: Non-critical functionality affected
- **Low**: Minor issue or question

**Case Type:**
- **Question**: General inquiry
- **Problem**: Technical issue requiring troubleshooting
- **Request**: Feature request or enhancement
- **Incident**: Service disruption or outage
- **Complaint**: Customer complaint
- **Bug**: Software defect
- **Feedback**: Customer feedback

**AI Intelligence:**
- **Auto-Categorization**: Classify case type automatically
- **Smart Assignment**: Route to best agent based on skills, workload, performance
- **Solution Recommendation**: Suggest knowledge articles for resolution
- **SLA Breach Prediction**: Predict which cases at risk of breach
- **Sentiment Analysis**: Gauge customer frustration level
- **Priority Prediction**: Recommend appropriate priority
- **Resolution Time Estimate**: Predict time to resolution

### SLA Management

Track and enforce service level agreements.

**Key Features:**
- **SLA Policies**: Define SLAs by customer tier and case priority
- **Milestone Tracking**: First response and resolution time
- **Business Hours**: Respect business hours for SLA calculation
- **Holiday Calendars**: Exclude holidays from SLA time
- **Breach Alerts**: Proactive alerts before SLA breach
- **Performance Reports**: SLA compliance tracking
- **Customer Tiers**: Different SLAs for different customer segments

**SLA Customer Tiers:**

**Platinum Tier** (Premium customers)
- First Response: 1 hour
- Resolution Time: 4 hours (Critical), 8 hours (High)
- 24/7 support coverage
- Dedicated account manager
- Priority escalation

**Gold Tier** (Key customers)
- First Response: 2 hours
- Resolution Time: 8 hours (Critical), 16 hours (High)
- Business hours + extended coverage
- Named support contact

**Silver Tier** (Standard customers)
- First Response: 4 hours
- Resolution Time: 1 business day (Critical), 2 days (High)
- Business hours support

**Bronze Tier** (Basic customers)
- First Response: 8 hours
- Resolution Time: 2 business days (Critical), 5 days (High)
- Business hours support
- Community forum available

**None** (No SLA commitment)
- Best effort support
- Community forum
- Self-service knowledge base

**SLA Milestones:**
- **First Response**: Time to first agent response
- **First Resolution Attempt**: Time to first solution provided
- **Final Resolution**: Time to case closure
- **Customer Response**: Time customer takes to respond

**Business Hours Configuration:**
- Define business hours by timezone
- Specify working days (Monday-Friday)
- Set start and end times
- Configure multiple shifts if 24/7
- Link to holiday calendars

**AI Intelligence:**
- **Breach Prediction**: Identify cases at risk of SLA breach
- **Resolution Time Prediction**: Estimate time needed to resolve
- **Workload Optimization**: Balance workload to meet SLAs
- **Escalation Recommendations**: When to escalate to meet SLA
- **Resource Planning**: Forecast staffing needs for SLA compliance

### Queue Management

Organize support teams and route cases efficiently.

**Key Features:**
- **Support Queues**: Organize cases by team or specialty
- **Queue Assignment**: Assign agents to queues
- **Load Balancing**: Distribute cases evenly
- **Skill-Based Routing**: Match cases to agent expertise
- **Round-Robin**: Sequential assignment
- **Overflow Handling**: Reassign when queue overloaded
- **Queue Metrics**: Monitor queue performance

**Queue Types:**
- **General Support**: Default queue for all cases
- **Technical Support**: Complex technical issues
- **Billing**: Billing and payment questions
- **Returns**: Return and refund requests
- **VIP**: Premium customer queue
- **Level 2**: Escalated cases
- **Product Teams**: Product-specific queues

**Queue Configuration:**
- Queue name and description
- Queue email address for email-to-case
- Business hours
- SLA policy
- Auto-assignment rules
- Escalation rules
- Queue members (agents)

**Routing Strategies:**
- **Skill-Based**: Route to agent with required skills
- **Load-Based**: Route to agent with lowest case count
- **Performance-Based**: Route to highest-performing agent
- **Round-Robin**: Rotate through available agents
- **Priority-First**: High-priority cases routed first
- **Hybrid**: Combination of multiple strategies

### Skill Management

Match cases to agents with the right expertise.

**Key Features:**
- **Skill Inventory**: Catalog of support skills
- **Agent Skills**: Skills each agent possesses
- **Proficiency Levels**: Beginner, Intermediate, Expert, Master
- **Skill Requirements**: Cases requiring specific skills
- **Skill-Based Routing**: Auto-assign based on skills
- **Skill Gap Analysis**: Identify training needs
- **Certification Tracking**: Track skill certifications

**Skill Categories:**
- **Product Knowledge**: Expertise with specific products
- **Technical Skills**: Programming, networking, databases
- **Language Skills**: English, Chinese, Spanish, etc.
- **Soft Skills**: Communication, empathy, problem-solving
- **Industry Knowledge**: Healthcare, finance, retail expertise
- **Tool Proficiency**: Specific software or tool expertise

**Skill Levels:**
- **Beginner (1)**: Basic understanding, needs supervision
- **Intermediate (2)**: Can handle common issues independently
- **Expert (3)**: Deep expertise, can handle complex issues
- **Master (4)**: Subject matter expert, trains others

**Routing Weight:**
- Higher proficiency = higher routing priority
- Expert-level agents get complex cases first
- Beginners get simpler cases for training
- Balance skill development with efficiency

## Support Workflows

### Case Resolution Workflow

Standard process for resolving customer issues:

1. **Case Creation**
   - Case received from any channel
   - Auto-categorization applied
   - Priority assigned
   - SLA timer started

2. **Case Assignment**
   - Routing rules determine queue
   - Skill-based matching to agent
   - Agent receives notification
   - Case appears in agent's queue

3. **Initial Response**
   - Agent reviews case details
   - Searches knowledge base
   - Sends first response to customer
   - First response SLA met

4. **Investigation**
   - Agent reproduces issue
   - Consults documentation
   - Collaborates with colleagues
   - Identifies root cause

5. **Solution Delivery**
   - Agent provides solution
   - Includes knowledge article if applicable
   - Requests customer confirmation
   - Updates case status to "Pending Customer"

6. **Customer Confirmation**
   - Customer confirms resolution or
   - Customer requests additional help
   - If resolved, case moved to "Resolved"

7. **Case Closure**
   - Agent closes case
   - Customer satisfaction survey sent
   - Case metrics captured
   - SLA compliance recorded

### Escalation Workflow

When a case needs escalation:

1. **Escalation Trigger**
   - SLA at risk of breach
   - Case complexity beyond agent skill
   - Customer requests escalation
   - Case not progressing

2. **Escalation Path**
   - Escalate to queue manager
   - Escalate to L2/L3 support
   - Escalate to product team
   - Escalate to management

3. **Escalation Handoff**
   - Complete escalation notes
   - Provide case context
   - Explain what's been tried
   - Transfer case ownership

4. **Resolution**
   - Escalated team resolves issue
   - Original agent notified
   - Customer updated
   - Case closed

### Knowledge Article Creation

From case to knowledge article:

1. **Identify Opportunity**
   - Recurring issue identified
   - Novel solution developed
   - Customer frequently asks

2. **Draft Article**
   - Agent or specialist writes article
   - Include step-by-step instructions
   - Add screenshots or videos
   - Use clear, simple language

3. **Review Process**
   - SME reviews for accuracy
   - Editor reviews for clarity
   - Manager approves

4. **Publish**
   - Article published to knowledge base
   - Linked to original case
   - Tagged appropriately
   - Made searchable

5. **Maintenance**
   - Regular reviews for accuracy
   - Update when product changes
   - Archive when obsolete

- **Reopened Cases**: Cases reopened after closure

- **Resolution SLA**: Compliance with resolution time
- **Breach Analysis**: Why SLAs were breached

- **Average Handle Time**: Efficiency by agent
- **Customer Satisfaction**: CSAT scores by agent
- **First Contact Resolution**: FCR rate by agent

- **Channel Efficiency**: Fastest channels to resolution
- **Channel Satisfaction**: CSAT by channel

## Best Practices

### Case Management Best Practices

1. **Fast First Response**: Respond quickly, even if just acknowledgment
2. **Clear Communication**: Use simple language, avoid jargon
3. **Document Everything**: Complete case history helps team
4. **Use Knowledge**: Link to articles, build institutional knowledge
5. **Follow Up**: Ensure customer satisfaction before closing

### SLA Management Best Practices

1. **Set Realistic SLAs**: Don't overpromise
2. **Monitor Closely**: Track SLA compliance in real-time
3. **Escalate Proactively**: Don't wait until breach
4. **Communicate Delays**: Keep customer informed
5. **Review Regularly**: Adjust SLAs based on performance

### Knowledge Base Best Practices

1. **Write Clearly**: Simple step-by-step instructions
2. **Use Screenshots**: Visual aids improve understanding
3. **Keep Current**: Review and update regularly
4. **SEO Optimize**: Make articles easy to find
5. **Measure Effectiveness**: Track which articles actually help

### Team Management Best Practices

1. **Skill Development**: Train agents continuously
2. **Fair Distribution**: Balance workload across team
3. **Quality Assurance**: Regular case reviews
4. **Recognize Excellence**: Reward top performers
5. **Share Learnings**: Team meetings to share insights
