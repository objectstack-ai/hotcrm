# Welcome to HotCRM

HotCRM is a **comprehensive, AI-native enterprise Customer Relationship Management system** that covers the complete business lifecycle from marketing to sales, service, finance, and human resources. Built on modern technology and designed with business users in mind, HotCRM delivers enterprise-level capabilities with an intuitive, beautiful user experience.

## What Makes HotCRM Different

### 1. AI-Native from the Ground Up

Every feature in HotCRM is enhanced with artificial intelligence:
- **Smart Lead Scoring**: Automatically prioritize your hottest leads with ML-powered scoring
- **Win Probability Prediction**: Know which deals to focus on with AI-driven forecasts
- **Intelligent Case Routing**: Route support tickets to the best agent automatically
- **Content Generation**: Create marketing content with AI assistance
- **Resume Parsing**: Extract candidate information instantly from resumes
- **Revenue Forecasting**: Predict future revenue with advanced analytics
