# Service Cloud

The **Service Cloud** module empowers support teams to resolve issues quickly and deliver personalized service at scale.

## Functional Capabilities

### 1. Case Management
Centralize support requests from Email, Phone, and Web.
- **Omni-Channel Routing**: Assign cases to agents based on availability and skills.
- **Milestones (SLAs)**: Track resolution times against service agreements.
- **Escalation Rules**: Automatically alert managers for overdue cases.

### 2. Knowledge Base
Deflect cases and empower agents with a searchable article library.
- **Article Versioning**: Manage content lifecycle.
- **Contextual Search**: AI-driven suggestions based on case subject.
- **Public Portal**: Expose articles to customers.

### 3. Customer Portal
Self-service community for customers to log tickets and view status.

## Integration Points

- **Sales Cloud**: View support history on Account/Contact.
- **Revenue Cloud**: Check entitlement/subscription status.
