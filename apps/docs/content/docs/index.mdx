## Key Features

### Sales Force Automation
- **Lead Management**: Capture, score, and convert leads with AI
- **Account & Contact 360**: Complete customer view
- **Opportunity Pipeline**: Manage deals with win probability
- **Activity Tracking**: Log and analyze customer interactions
- **Quote & Proposal**: Configure, price, and quote (CPQ)

### Marketing Automation
- **Campaign Management**: Plan and execute multi-channel campaigns
- **Email Marketing**: Design and send professional emails
- **Landing Pages**: Create high-converting pages
- **Marketing Lists**: Segment and target audiences
- **AI Content Generation**: Create marketing content with AI

### Customer Service
- **Omnichannel Case Management**: Handle tickets from any channel
- **SLA Tracking**: Monitor and enforce service levels
- **Knowledge Base**: Self-service and agent knowledge
- **Intelligent Routing**: AI-powered case assignment
- **Customer Satisfaction**: Track and improve CSAT

### Products & Revenue
- **Product Catalog**: Comprehensive product management
- **Dynamic Pricing**: Flexible pricing strategies
- **Quote Configuration**: Advanced CPQ capabilities
- **Contract Management**: Full contract lifecycle
- **Invoice & Payment**: Billing and payment tracking

### Human Resources
- **Recruitment (ATS)**: Complete applicant tracking
- **Onboarding**: New hire integration
- **Employee Management**: Central employee records
- **Performance Management**: Reviews and goal tracking
- **Time & Attendance**: Leave and attendance tracking

## System Overview

### Platform Statistics

- **65 Core Business Objects** covering complete enterprise operations
- **23 AI-Powered Actions** providing intelligent automation
- **29 Automation Hooks** for business process automation
- **6 Major Business Clouds** (Sales, Marketing, Products, Finance, Service, HR)
- **8+ Currencies** for global operations
- **Multi-Channel Support** (Email, Web, Phone, Social, Chat, Mobile)

### Technical Architecture

- **Metadata-Driven**: All objects defined in TypeScript
- **Plugin Architecture**: Modular, independent business packages
- **ObjectQL**: Type-safe query language
- **AI-Native**: ML/AI integrated throughout
- **Modern Stack**: Built on @objectstack/runtime platform
- **REST APIs**: Complete API access for integrations
