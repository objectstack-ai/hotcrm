# Finance & Revenue Cloud

The HotCRM Finance & Revenue Cloud manages the complete revenue lifecycle from contract execution through invoicing and payment collection, with AI-powered revenue forecasting and risk management.

## Core Capabilities

### Contract Management

Manage the complete contract lifecycle from creation to renewal.

**Key Features:**
- **Contract Creation**: Generate contracts from accepted quotes
- **Contract Repository**: Centralized contract storage
- **Lifecycle Management**: Draft → Activated → Completed flow
- **Effective Dating**: Contract start and end dates
- **Auto-Renewal**: Automatic renewal with notification
- **Amendment Tracking**: Contract changes and revisions
- **E-Signature Integration**: Digital signature ready
- **Document Storage**: Store signed contracts and attachments
- **Renewal Reminders**: Proactive renewal alerts
- **Contract Value Tracking**: Total contract value (TCV)

**Contract Status:**

1. **Draft**: Contract being prepared
   - Terms being negotiated
   - Not yet effective
   - Can be edited freely

2. **Pending Approval**: Awaiting signatures
   - Sent to customer for signature
   - Pending internal approvals
   - Cannot edit without voiding

3. **Activated**: Contract in effect
   - Signed by all parties
   - Services being delivered
   - Billing active

4. **Completed**: Contract fulfilled
   - End date reached
   - All obligations met
   - Historical record

5. **Cancelled**: Contract terminated
   - Terminated before completion
   - Cancellation reason captured
   - Billing stopped

**Contract Types:**
- **Service Agreement**: Ongoing services contract
- **Subscription**: Recurring revenue (monthly, annual)
- **One-Time**: Single delivery contract
- **Maintenance**: Support and maintenance agreement
- **License**: Software licensing agreement
- **Partnership**: Channel partner agreements
- **Master Service Agreement (MSA)**: Framework for future work

**Key Contract Fields:**
- Contract number (auto-generated)
- Account and opportunity links
- Contract value and currency
- Payment terms (Net 30, Net 60, etc.)
- Billing frequency (monthly, quarterly, annual)
- Service level agreement (SLA)
- Contract owner (account manager)
- Renewal date and terms
- Cancellation clause

**Billing Automation:**
- **Auto-Invoice Generation**: Create invoices on contract activation
- **Recurring Billing**: Schedule automatic invoice creation
- **Milestone Billing**: Invoice at project milestones
- **Usage-Based Billing**: Invoice based on consumption
- **Subscription Billing**: Automatic renewal billing

**AI Intelligence:**
- **Risk Scoring**: Identify high-risk contracts
- **Renewal Prediction**: Predict likelihood of renewal
- **Churn Risk**: Identify at-risk renewals
- **Compliance Checking**: Flag non-standard terms
- **Value Optimization**: Suggest contract improvements
- **Upsell Opportunities**: Identify expansion possibilities

### Invoice Management

Generate, track, and manage customer invoices.

**Key Features:**
- **Invoice Generation**: Create from contracts or quotes
- **Invoice Templates**: Professional invoice layouts
- **Line Item Detail**: Detailed billing breakdown
- **Tax Calculation**: Automatic tax computation
- **Multi-Currency**: Invoice in customer's currency
- **Payment Terms**: Net 30, Net 60, Due on Receipt
- **PDF Generation**: Professional invoice documents
- **Email Delivery**: Send invoices directly to customers
- **Payment Tracking**: Link payments to invoices
- **Aging Reports**: Overdue invoice tracking

**Invoice Status:**

1. **Draft**: Invoice being prepared
2. **Sent**: Invoice sent to customer
3. **Partially Paid**: Partial payment received
4. **Paid**: Fully paid
5. **Overdue**: Past due date
6. **Cancelled**: Invoice voided
7. **In Collections**: Sent to collections

**Invoice Types:**
- **Standard Invoice**: Regular billing invoice
- **Credit Memo**: Refund or credit
- **Debit Memo**: Additional charges
- **Proforma**: Estimate, not official invoice
- **Recurring**: Subscription or recurring charges
- **Milestone**: Project milestone billing
- **Final**: Project completion billing

**Payment Terms:**
- **Due on Receipt**: Immediate payment
- **Net 15**: Payment due in 15 days
- **Net 30**: Payment due in 30 days
- **Net 60**: Payment due in 60 days
- **Net 90**: Payment due in 90 days
- **Custom**: Negotiated terms

**Invoice Components:**
- Invoice number (auto-generated)
- Invoice date and due date
- Bill-to and ship-to addresses
- Line items with descriptions
- Quantity, unit price, extended amount
- Subtotal, tax, shipping, total
- Payment instructions
- Terms and conditions

**AI Intelligence:**
- **Payment Default Prediction**: Identify likely late payers
- **Cash Flow Forecasting**: Predict payment timing
- **Collection Priority**: Which invoices to pursue first
- **Payment Plan Suggestions**: Offer payment arrangements
- **Dispute Risk**: Flag potential billing disputes

### Invoice Line Items

Detailed breakdown of invoiced products and services.

**Key Features:**
- **Product Reference**: Link to product catalog
- **Service Details**: Description of services
- **Quantity and Units**: Amount billed
- **Unit Price**: Price per unit
- **Extended Amount**: Quantity × Unit Price
- **Discounts**: Line-level discounts
- **Tax**: Product-specific tax rates
- **Service Dates**: Service period for subscription items

**Line Item Types:**
- **Product**: Physical goods
- **Service**: Professional services or labor
- **Subscription**: Recurring subscription fee
- **License**: Software or IP license
- **Support**: Maintenance or support fee
- **Shipping**: Freight and delivery
- **Tax**: Tax line items

### Payment Management

Track and manage customer payments.

**Key Features:**
- **Payment Recording**: Log payment receipts
- **Payment Methods**: Cash, check, credit card, wire transfer, ACH
- **Payment Application**: Apply payments to invoices
- **Partial Payments**: Handle payment installments
- **Payment Plans**: Set up payment schedules
- **Refund Processing**: Process customer refunds
- **Payment Status**: Track payment lifecycle
- **Bank Reconciliation**: Match payments to bank deposits
- **Payment Reminders**: Automated payment reminders

**Payment Types:**

1. **Down Payment**: Initial deposit
   - Percentage of total contract value
   - Due upon contract signing
   - Applied to final payment

2. **Milestone Payment**: Project-based payments
   - Tied to project milestones
   - Invoiced upon completion
   - Progress-based billing

3. **Recurring Payment**: Subscription payments
   - Monthly, quarterly, or annual
   - Auto-charged if authorized
   - Predictable revenue

4. **Final Payment**: Project completion
   - Balance due upon delivery
   - After down payment and milestones
   - Closes out contract

**Payment Status:**
- **Pending**: Payment expected but not received
- **Received**: Payment received, not yet cleared
- **Cleared**: Payment cleared the bank
- **Applied**: Payment applied to invoices
- **Refunded**: Payment returned to customer
- **Failed**: Payment transaction failed

**Payment Methods:**
- **Cash**: Physical cash payment
- **Check**: Paper check
- **Credit Card**: Visa, Mastercard, Amex
- **Wire Transfer**: Bank wire
- **ACH**: Electronic check
- **PayPal**: Online payment
- **Stripe**: Online payment processor

**Payment Collection:**
- Send payment reminders at intervals
- Escalate overdue accounts
- Assign to collections
- Track collection status
- Calculate collection costs
- Report on collection effectiveness

**AI Intelligence:**
- Predict payment date before it occurs
- Identify customers likely to default
- Suggest optimal payment plans
- Prioritize collection efforts
- Forecast cash flow by week/month

## Finance Workflows

### Quote-to-Cash Workflow

Complete revenue lifecycle from quote to payment:

1. **Quote Creation**
   - Sales creates quote for opportunity
   - Products and pricing configured
   - Quote approved and sent to customer

2. **Quote Acceptance**
   - Customer accepts quote
   - Quote marked as accepted
   - Opportunity moved to Closed Won

3. **Contract Creation**
   - System auto-creates contract from quote
   - Contract terms populated
   - Contract sent for signature

4. **Contract Activation**
   - Contract signed by all parties
   - Contract status set to Activated
   - Billing schedule established

5. **Invoice Generation**
   - Invoice auto-created based on contract
   - Invoice sent to customer
   - Payment terms communicated

6. **Payment Collection**
   - Customer sends payment
   - Payment recorded in system
   - Payment applied to invoice

7. **Revenue Recognition**
   - Revenue recognized per accounting rules
   - Financial reports updated
   - Contract fulfilled or renewed

### Recurring Billing Workflow

Automated subscription billing:

1. **Contract Setup**
   - Subscription contract created
   - Billing frequency set (monthly, annual)
   - First invoice date established

2. **Automatic Invoice Creation**
   - System creates invoice on schedule
   - Invoice sent to customer automatically
   - Payment reminder scheduled

3. **Auto-Payment Processing** (if authorized)
   - Payment charged to customer's card/account
   - Payment recorded automatically
   - Receipt sent to customer

4. **Renewal Processing**
   - Contract approaching renewal
   - Renewal notice sent to customer
   - Customer confirms or cancels
   - Contract renewed or closed

### Collections Workflow

Process for overdue invoice collection:

1. **Payment Reminder** (5 days before due)
   - Friendly reminder email
   - Invoice attached
   - Payment instructions included

2. **First Notice** (Due date)
   - Payment due notification
   - Request immediate payment
   - Offer payment plan if needed

3. **Second Notice** (7 days overdue)
   - More urgent tone
   - Late fee notice
   - Payment plan offer

4. **Final Notice** (14 days overdue)
   - Final warning before collections
   - Account suspension notice
   - Legal action warning

5. **Collections Assignment** (30 days overdue)
   - Assigned to collections team or agency
   - Collection efforts begin
   - Account status updated

6. **Resolution**
   - Payment received or
   - Payment plan established or
   - Written off as bad debt

## Finance Analytics & Reporting

### Revenue Metrics
- **Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)**: Subscription revenue per month
- **Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR)**: Annualized subscription revenue
- **Total Contract Value (TCV)**: Sum of all active contracts
- **Average Contract Value (ACV)**: Mean contract value
- **Revenue by Product**: Product mix analysis
- **Revenue by Customer**: Customer concentration
- **Revenue Growth Rate**: Period-over-period growth

### Collection Metrics
- **Days Sales Outstanding (DSO)**: Average collection time
- **Collection Rate**: Percentage of invoices collected
- **Aging Report**: Invoices by age (current, 30, 60, 90+ days)
- **Write-Off Rate**: Bad debt percentage
- **Collection Efficiency**: Cost to collect vs. collected amount

### Cash Flow Metrics
- **Cash Flow Forecast**: Projected cash receipts
- **Payment Velocity**: How fast customers pay
- **Revenue Recognition**: Earned vs. billed revenue
- **Deferred Revenue**: Prepaid but not yet earned
- **Unbilled Revenue**: Earned but not yet invoiced

### Contract Metrics
- **Contract Renewal Rate**: Percentage renewed
- **Churn Rate**: Percentage cancelled
- **Expansion Revenue**: Upsells and cross-sells to existing customers
- **Net Retention**: Renewal + expansion - churn
- **Contract Lifetime Value (CLV)**: Total value over contract life

### Invoice Metrics
- **Invoices Sent**: Volume by period
- **Invoice Value**: Total invoiced amount
- **Payment Rate**: Percentage paid on time
- **Dispute Rate**: Percentage of invoices disputed
- **Time to Payment**: Invoice date to payment date

## Best Practices

### Contract Management Best Practices

1. **Standard Templates**: Use pre-approved contract templates
2. **Clear Terms**: No ambiguity in contract language
3. **Fast Execution**: Minimize time from quote to contract
4. **Digital Signatures**: Accelerate contract signing
5. **Renewal Tracking**: Never miss a renewal opportunity

### Invoicing Best Practices

1. **Invoice Promptly**: Send invoices immediately upon delivery
2. **Clear Details**: Itemized billing prevents disputes
3. **Professional Format**: Use clean, branded templates
4. **Multiple Payment Options**: Make it easy to pay
5. **Follow Up**: Don't let invoices sit unpaid

### Payment Collection Best Practices

1. **Set Expectations**: Communicate payment terms clearly
2. **Remind Early**: Don't wait until overdue
3. **Make It Easy**: Multiple payment methods
4. **Be Persistent**: Polite but consistent follow-up
5. **Offer Options**: Payment plans for large amounts

### Revenue Recognition Best Practices

1. **Follow Standards**: Comply with accounting standards (ASC 606, IFRS 15)
2. **Accurate Tracking**: Deferred revenue and unbilled revenue
3. **Regular Review**: Monthly reconciliation
4. **Audit Trail**: Document recognition decisions
5. **System Integration**: Integrate with accounting system
