# Sales Cloud

The **Sales Cloud** module is designed to help sales teams sell faster and smarter. It centralizes customer information, automates interactions, and provides intelligence to drive deal closure.

## Functional Capabilities

### 1. Lead Management
Capture and qualify leads from multiple channels.
- **Web-to-Lead**: Automatically capture leads from your website.
- **Lead Scoring**: Prioritize leads based on engagement and demographics.
- **Assignment Rules**: Automatically route leads to the right reps.

### 2. Opportunity Management
Manage the deal lifecycle with a visual pipeline.
- **Kanban Board**: Drag-and-drop deals across stages.
- **Sales Path**: Step-by-step guidance for each sales stage.
- **Deal Health**: AI insight into deal probability.

### 3. Forecasting & Performance
Predict revenue and track team performance.
- **Roll-up Forecasting**: View pipeline from individual rep to VP level.
- **Quota Tracking**: Monitor progress against targets.

## Integration Points

- **Marketing Cloud**: Receives qualified leads.
- **Revenue Cloud**: Sends closed deals for quoting/billing.
