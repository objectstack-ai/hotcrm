## Complete Business Platform

HotCRM provides **6 major business clouds** covering the entire customer lifecycle, all built on the same metadata-driven foundation.

## Business Clouds

### üéØ Sales Cloud (CRM)

Complete B2B and B2C customer management with AI-powered insights.

**13 Objects | 8 AI Actions | 7 Automation Hooks**

**Core Capabilities:**
- **Account Management**: Enterprise customer & organization tracking with health scoring
- **Contact Management**: Individual relationship tracking with decision maker identification
- **Lead Management**: AI-native lead scoring, enrichment, and duplicate detection
- **Opportunity Pipeline**: 7-stage sales pipeline with win probability forecasting
- **Activity Tracking**: Calls, emails, meetings with sentiment analysis
- **Email Marketing**: Templates, landing pages, forms with A/B testing
- **Marketing Lists**: Dynamic segmentation with GDPR compliance

## Cross-Cutting Capabilities

### ü§ñ AI-Native Features

Every package includes AI capabilities:

- **Predictive Scoring**: Lead scoring, candidate ranking, product recommendations
- **Content Generation**: Email templates, social posts, knowledge articles
- **Data Enrichment**: Auto-fill company data, contact information
- **Anomaly Detection**: Payment defaults, unusual patterns
- **Sentiment Analysis**: Customer satisfaction, employee sentiment

### üìä Analytics & Reporting

- **Real-time Dashboards**: Live metrics for all business objects
- **Custom Reports**: Build reports using ObjectQL
- **Forecasting**: Revenue, pipeline, hiring predictions
- **Trend Analysis**: Historical data analysis and pattern detection

### üîí Security & Compliance

- **Field-Level Permissions**: Granular access control
- **Audit Trails**: Complete change history for all records
- **Data Encryption**: At-rest and in-transit encryption
- **GDPR Compliance**: Data privacy and right-to-be-forgotten

- **ObjectQL Access**: Programmatic data access
