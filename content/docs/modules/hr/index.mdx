# HR Cloud

The **HR Cloud** module provides a complete Human Capital Management (HCM) solution, covering the entire employee lifecycle from recruitment to retirement. Built on the same metadata-driven architecture as our other clouds, it delivers enterprise-grade HR functionality with AI augmentation.

## Functional Capabilities

### 1. Recruitment & Talent Acquisition

Streamline your hiring process from job posting to offer acceptance.

- **Position Management**: Define open positions with requirements and budgets
- **Candidate Pipeline**: Track applicants through interview stages
- **Application Tracking**: Centralized candidate database with resume parsing
- **Interview Scheduling**: Coordinate multi-round interviews with feedback capture
- **Offer Management**: Generate and track offer letters with e-signature support

**Objects**: `position`, `candidate`, `application`, `interview`, `offer`, `recruitment`

### 2. Onboarding & Offboarding

Ensure smooth employee transitions with structured workflows.

- **Onboarding Checklists**: Automated task assignment for new hires
- **Asset Management**: Track laptops, access cards, and equipment
- **Training Enrollment**: Auto-assign required courses
- **Compliance Documentation**: I-9, W-4, and policy acknowledgments

**Objects**: `onboarding`, `employee`

### 3. Time & Attendance

Track work hours, time-off, and attendance with precision.

- **Time Off Requests**: PTO, sick leave, and vacation management
- **Attendance Tracking**: Clock in/out with location verification
- **Holiday Calendars**: Regional and company-specific holiday management
- **Overtime Calculations**: Automatic compliance with labor regulations

**Objects**: `time_off`, `attendance`, `holiday`, `holiday_calendar`

### 4. Performance Management

Drive employee growth with continuous feedback and goal tracking.

- **Goal Setting**: OKR and KPI frameworks with cascading goals
- **Performance Reviews**: 360-degree feedback and annual reviews
- **Development Plans**: Personalized growth trajectories
- **Performance Improvement Plans (PIP)**: Structured support for struggling employees

**Objects**: `goal`, `performance_review`

### 5. Compensation & Payroll

Manage salaries, bonuses, and benefits with accuracy.

- **Payroll Processing**: Automated salary calculations with tax withholding
- **Bonus Management**: Performance-based incentive tracking
- **Benefits Administration**: Health, dental, retirement plan enrollment
- **Salary Reviews**: Merit increase workflows with approval chains

**Objects**: `payroll`, `employee`

### 6. Learning & Development

Invest in your workforce with comprehensive training programs.

- **Training Catalog**: Course library with prerequisites and certifications
- **Enrollment Management**: Track employee skill development
- **Certification Tracking**: Monitor compliance certifications and renewals
- **Skills Matrix**: Identify skill gaps and training needs

**Objects**: `training`, `certification`

### 7. Organization Structure

Define your company hierarchy and departmental structure.

- **Department Management**: Organizational units with cost centers
- **Reporting Lines**: Manager-employee relationships with matrix support
- **Headcount Planning**: Budgeted vs. actual FTE tracking

**Objects**: `department`, `employee`

## AI-Powered Features

Every HR process is enhanced with intelligent automation:

- **Resume Parsing**: Automatically extract candidate information
- **Interview Question Generation**: Role-specific questions based on job description
- **Performance Review Insights**: AI-detected patterns in feedback
- **Predictive Attrition**: Early warning system for employee retention risks
- **Skills Gap Analysis**: AI recommendations for training programs
- **Compensation Benchmarking**: Market-based salary recommendations

## Integration Points

- **CRM Cloud**: Link employees to customer accounts for relationship mapping
- **Support Cloud**: Employee self-service portal for HR tickets
- **Finance Cloud**: Payroll integration with accounting systems
