# Subscription Billing

Automate the lifecycle of recurring revenue. Move beyond one-time sales to a subscription economy model.

## Features

### Recurring Billing
- **Frequencies**: Monthly, Quarterly, Annual, or Custom.
- **Proration**: Automatically calculate partial charges for mid-month signups or upgrades.
- **Invoicing**: Generate and email invoices automatically on billing cycles.

### Lifecycle Management
- **Upgrades/Downgrades**: Handle plan changes seamlessly, calculating credits and new charges instantly.
- **Renewals**: Auto-create renewal opportunities 90 days before contract expiry.
- **Churn Prevention**: Automated dunning emails for failed credit card payments.

### Revenue Recognition
Recognize revenue when it is earned, not just when cash is received.
- **ASC 606 Compliance**: Automate revenue schedules.
- **Deferred Revenue**: Track unearned revenue liability.

## Key Metrics
- **MRR / ARR**: Monthly/Annual Recurring Revenue.
- **Churn Rate**: Percentage of customers lost.
- **CLV**: Customer Lifetime Value.
