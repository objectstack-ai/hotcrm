# Human Capital Management Cloud

The HotCRM Human Capital Management (HCM) Cloud manages the complete employee lifecycle from recruitment through retirement, with AI-powered talent acquisition, performance management, and workforce analytics.

## Core Capabilities

### Recruitment & Talent Acquisition

Streamline hiring from job posting through offer acceptance.

**Key Features:**
- **Position Management**: Job requisition tracking
- **Applicant Tracking System (ATS)**: Complete candidate pipeline
- **Resume Parsing**: AI extraction of candidate information
- **Interview Scheduling**: Multi-round interview coordination
- **Offer Management**: Digital offer letters and tracking
- **Candidate Communication**: Automated status updates
- **Hiring Workflow**: Structured hiring process
- **Recruitment Analytics**: Source effectiveness, time-to-hire

### Position Management

Define and track open positions.

**Key Features:**
- **Job Requisitions**: Formal request to hire
- **Position Details**: Title, department, reporting structure
- **Requirements**: Skills, experience, education needed
- **Salary Range**: Budget for position
- **Headcount Tracking**: Approved vs. filled positions
- **Position Status**: Open, on hold, filled, cancelled
- **Approval Workflow**: Manager → HR → Finance approval

**Position Types:**
- **Full-Time**: Standard full-time employment
- **Part-Time**: Part-time employment
- **Contract**: Fixed-term contract
- **Temporary**: Short-term temporary
- **Intern**: Internship program
- **Consultant**: External consultant

**Position Fields:**
- Position title and job family
- Department and cost center
- Hiring manager
- Salary range (min, max)
- Required skills and experience
- Education requirements
- Employment type
- Number of openings
- Target start date
- Job description

### Candidate Management

Track candidates through the hiring pipeline.

**Key Features:**
- **Candidate Profiles**: Complete candidate information
- **Resume Storage**: Store and parse resumes
- **Source Tracking**: Where candidates came from
- **Stage Progression**: Track through hiring stages
- **Communication History**: All interactions logged
- **Skill Matching**: Match candidates to positions
- **Duplicate Detection**: Prevent duplicate candidates

**Candidate Sources:**
- **Job Board**: Indeed, LinkedIn, Glassdoor
- **Referral**: Employee referral
- **Company Website**: Direct application
- **Recruiter**: Agency or headhunter
- **Social Media**: LinkedIn, Twitter recruiting
- **University**: Campus recruiting
- **Internal**: Internal candidate
- **Direct**: Direct sourcing by recruiter

**Candidate Fields:**
- Name and contact information
- Current employer and title
- Years of experience
- Highest education level
- Resume and cover letter
- LinkedIn profile
- Salary expectations
- Availability to start
- Willing to relocate
- Work authorization

**AI Intelligence:**
- **Resume Parsing**: Extract information from resumes automatically
- **Candidate Matching**: Score candidates against position requirements
- **Candidate Ranking**: Rank candidates by fit
- **Interview Questions**: Generate tailored interview questions
- **Skills Gap**: Identify training needed
- **Success Prediction**: Predict candidate success probability

### Application Tracking

Track candidate applications and progression.

**Key Features:**
- **Application Status**: Track through hiring stages
- **Stage Workflow**: Structured progression
- **Rejection Reasons**: Capture why candidates rejected
- **Application History**: Complete timeline
- **Bulk Actions**: Move multiple candidates at once
- **Automated Emails**: Status update notifications

**Application Stages:**
1. **Applied**: Application received
2. **Screening**: Resume review
3. **Phone Screen**: Initial phone interview
4. **Interview**: In-person or video interview
5. **Technical Assessment**: Skills testing
6. **Final Interview**: Interview with leadership
7. **Offer Extended**: Offer letter sent
8. **Offer Accepted**: Candidate accepted
9. **Offer Declined**: Candidate declined
10. **Rejected**: Candidate not selected

**Rejection Reasons:**
- **Qualifications**: Doesn't meet requirements
- **Experience**: Insufficient experience
- **Skills**: Lacks required skills
- **Culture Fit**: Not a culture fit
- **Salary**: Salary expectations too high
- **Location**: Won't relocate
- **Other Opportunity**: Accepted other offer
- **Withdrew**: Candidate withdrew

Schedule and track interviews.

**Key Features:**

- **Interview Types**: Phone, video, in-person, technical

Create and track employment offers.

**Key Features:**
- **Offer Templates**: Standard offer letter templates
- **Offer Details**: Salary, bonus, equity, benefits
- **Probation Period**: Probationary period definition
- **Start Date**: Proposed start date
- **Offer Status**: Extended, accepted, declined, expired
- **E-Signature**: Digital signature integration

- **Negotiation Tracking**: Offer modifications

**Offer Components:**
- **Base Salary**: Annual base compensation
- **Sign-On Bonus**: One-time signing bonus

- **Benefits**: Health, dental, retirement

**Offer Status:**

**Offer Lifecycle:**
1. Hiring manager prepares offer
2. HR reviews and approves
3. Finance approves budget
4. Offer sent to candidate
5. Candidate accepts/declines/negotiates
6. If accepted, onboarding begins

### Payroll Management

Process employee compensation.

**Key Features:**
- **Salary Processing**: Monthly salary calculation
- **Payroll Components**: Base, bonus, overtime, deductions
- **Deductions**: Tax, insurance, retirement contributions
- **Net Pay Calculation**: Gross pay minus deductions
- **Payment Methods**: Bank transfer, check, cash
- **Payslip Generation**: Digital payslips
- **Payroll Reports**: Payroll summaries and tax reports

**Payroll Components:**

**Earnings:**
- Base salary
- Overtime pay
- Performance bonus
- Sales commission
- Allowances (housing, transport, meal)
- Incentives

**Deductions:**
- Income tax
- Social security
- Health insurance
- Retirement contribution
- Loan repayment
- Advances

**Payroll Calculation:**
1. Gross pay = Base + Overtime + Bonus + Allowances
2. Total deductions = Tax + Insurance + Retirement + Other
3. Net pay = Gross pay - Total deductions

**Payroll Status:**
- **Draft**: Being calculated
- **Pending Approval**: Awaiting approval
- **Approved**: Approved for payment
- **Paid**: Payment completed
- **Failed**: Payment failed

## HR Workflows

### Recruitment Workflow

Complete hiring process:

1. **Position Requisition**
   - Manager creates position request
   - HR reviews and approves
   - Finance approves budget
   - Position opened for recruiting

2. **Job Posting**
   - Job description created
   - Posted to job boards
   - Shared on social media
   - Employee referrals solicited

3. **Candidate Sourcing**
   - Applications received
   - Resumes parsed
   - Candidates screened
   - Top candidates identified

4. **Interview Process**
   - Phone screens scheduled
   - In-person interviews coordinated
   - Technical assessments
   - Final interviews
   - Feedback collected

5. **Offer Extension**
   - Offer prepared
   - Approvals obtained
   - Offer sent to candidate
   - Negotiation if needed
   - Offer accepted

6. **Onboarding**
   - Background check
   - Paperwork completed
   - Equipment provisioned
   - First day scheduled
   - Onboarding begins

### Performance Review Workflow

Annual performance review process:

1. **Review Cycle Launch**
   - HR announces review period
   - Timeline communicated
   - Review forms distributed

2. **Self-Assessment**
   - Employee completes self-assessment
   - Reflects on accomplishments
   - Identifies development needs

3. **Manager Assessment**
   - Manager reviews employee performance
   - Rates on competencies
   - Writes narrative feedback
   - Recommends rating and raise

4. **360-Degree Feedback** (if applicable)
   - Peers provide feedback
   - Direct reports provide feedback (for managers)
   - Feedback synthesized

5. **Review Meeting**
   - Manager and employee meet
   - Discuss performance
   - Review feedback
   - Set goals for next period
   - Discuss development

6. **Calibration**
   - Managers meet to calibrate ratings
   - Ensure consistency across team
   - Adjust ratings if needed

7. **Final Approval**
   - HR reviews and approves
   - Compensation changes processed
   - Review finalized in system

### Termination Workflow

Employee separation process:

1. **Resignation/Termination**
   - Employee resigns or is terminated
   - Notice period determined
   - Termination record created

2. **Exit Preparation**
   - Knowledge transfer planned
   - Replacement sourced if needed
   - Exit tasks assigned

3. **Exit Interview**
   - HR conducts exit interview
   - Feedback collected
   - Insights captured

4. **Offboarding**
   - Access revoked
   - Equipment returned
   - Final paycheck processed
   - Benefits transition explained

5. **Exit Complete**
   - Employee record updated
   - Documentation completed
   - Alumni status assigned (if applicable)

## Best Practices

### Recruitment Best Practices

1. **Clear Job Descriptions**: Detailed, accurate requirements
2. **Fast Hiring**: Don't lose good candidates to slow process
3. **Candidate Experience**: Treat candidates professionally
4. **Structured Interviews**: Consistent evaluation criteria
5. **Sell the Role**: Candidates interview you too

### Onboarding Best Practices

1. **Start Before Day 1**: Engage before first day
2. **Structured Program**: Clear 90-day plan
3. **Assign a Buddy**: Experienced employee guide
4. **Regular Check-Ins**: Don't let new hires struggle alone
5. **Set Them Up for Success**: Clear goals and expectations

### Performance Management Best Practices

1. **Regular Feedback**: Don't wait for annual review
2. **SMART Goals**: Specific, measurable, achievable, relevant, time-bound
3. **Two-Way Conversation**: Listen to employee input
4. **Focus on Development**: Not just evaluation
5. **Document Everything**: Maintain written records

### Retention Best Practices

1. **Competitive Compensation**: Pay fairly and competitively
2. **Career Development**: Clear growth paths
3. **Recognition**: Appreciate good work
4. **Work-Life Balance**: Support flexibility
5. **Act on Feedback**: Address issues raised
