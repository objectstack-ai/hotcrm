# HotCRM Roadmap

HotCRM is evolving rapidly. Having established the core **6-Cloud Architecture** (Sales, Marketing, Revenue, Service, HR, AI) with **65 production-ready objects**, our focus shifts towards deeper intelligence, broader integration, and enhancing the developer experience.

## ‚úÖ Phase 1: Core Foundation (Completed)

We have successfully established the metadata-driven runtime and the core business logic for all primary clouds.

### Business Clouds (6 Completed)
- **Sales Cloud** ‚úÖ: 13 objects - Lead-to-Cash capability, Pipeline management, Opportunity lifecycle, Activity tracking
- **Marketing Cloud** ‚úÖ: 2 objects + 5 CRM marketing objects - Campaign management, Lead nurturing, Email automation
- **Revenue Cloud** ‚úÖ: 13 objects - CPQ (Configure-Price-Quote), Contract lifecycle, Invoice & Payment tracking
- **Service Cloud** ‚úÖ: 21 objects - Case management, SLA tracking, Knowledge base, Omni-channel routing
- **HR Cloud** ‚úÖ: 16 objects - Recruitment, Onboarding, Performance, Payroll, Training
- **AI Cloud** ‚úÖ: Unified AI service layer with 5 pre-registered ML models

### Platform Foundation (Completed)
- **Metadata Engine** ‚úÖ: TypeScript-native object definitions (`.object.ts`)
- **ObjectQL** ‚úÖ: Type-safe query language replacing SQL
- **Plugin Architecture** ‚úÖ: Independent packages with dependency management
- **Model Registry** ‚úÖ: Centralized ML model configuration and versioning
- **Prediction Service** ‚úÖ: Unified interface for all ML predictions with caching

### Total Delivered
- **65 Core Objects** across 6 business clouds
- **500+ Fields** for comprehensive data capture
- **5 AI Models** pre-registered and production-ready
- **8 Currency Support** for global operations
