# Quick Start Guide

Welcome to HotCRM! This guide will help you get up and running quickly. Whether you're a sales rep, marketer, support agent, or HR professional, you'll find what you need to start being productive right away.

## For Sales Reps

### Your First Week with HotCRM

#### Day 1: Get Oriented

**1. Set Up Your Profile**
- Log in to HotCRM
- Complete your user profile
- Upload a profile photo
- Set your notification preferences

**2. Explore Your Dashboard**
- View your sales pipeline
- Check open opportunities
- Review your tasks for the day
- See your performance metrics

**3. Learn the Basics**
- Navigate the main menu
- Search for records
- Create your first activity log
- Familiarize yourself with the interface

#### Day 2: Start with Leads

**1. Review Your Lead Queue**
- Access your assigned leads
- Sort by lead score (highest first)
- Review lead details and company info
- Check lead source and score

**2. Contact Your First Lead**
- Call or email your top-scored lead
- Log the activity in HotCRM
- Update lead status based on conversation
- Schedule next follow-up

**3. Convert a Qualified Lead**
- Find a qualified lead (BANT criteria met)
- Click "Convert Lead"
- System creates Account, Contact, and Opportunity
- Begin working the opportunity

#### Day 3: Manage Opportunities

**1. Review Your Pipeline**
- View all open opportunities
- Sort by close date or amount
- Identify opportunities needing attention
- Update stages as appropriate

**2. Update an Opportunity**
- Open an opportunity record
- Update stage if progress made
- Set/update close date
- Add notes on recent activities
- Set next steps

**3. Use AI Insights**
- Check AI win probability
- Review risk factors
- Read next step recommendations
- Follow AI talking points in next call

#### Day 4: Activity Tracking

**1. Log All Activities**
- Log every call, email, meeting
- Include outcome and next steps
- Attach relevant documents
- Set follow-up tasks

**2. Plan Your Day**
- Review today's tasks
- Prioritize by AI recommendations
- Block time for top opportunities
- Schedule calls and meetings

**3. Collaborate with Team**
- @mention colleagues in notes
- Share insights on accounts
- Ask for help on complex deals
- Celebrate wins with team

#### Day 5: Reports and Planning

**1. Review Your Performance**
- Check your pipeline value
- Review activities this week
- See conversion rates
- Compare to quota

**2. Plan Next Week**
- Identify opportunities to advance
- Schedule customer meetings
- Plan prospecting activities
- Set weekly goals

**3. Learn Advanced Features**
- Explore AI Smart Briefing
- Try voice transcription
- Use quote creation
- Experiment with automation

### Quick Reference: Common Tasks

**Create a Lead:**
1. Click "New Lead"
2. Fill in contact information
3. Set lead source
4. Save - system auto-scores

**Log a Call:**
1. Open Account/Contact/Opportunity
2. Click "Log Activity"
3. Select "Call" type
4. Enter duration and outcome
5. Set next follow-up

**Create an Opportunity:**
1. Click "New Opportunity"
2. Select Account
3. Enter opportunity details
4. Set close date and amount
5. Select sales stage

**Update Opportunity Stage:**
1. Open opportunity
2. Change stage dropdown
3. Update close date if needed
4. Add notes on why stage changed
5. Save

## Common Workflows

### Lead to Cash Workflow

1. **Lead Captured** → Marketing form submission
2. **Lead Scored** → AI scores lead automatically
3. **Lead Assigned** → Routed to sales rep
4. **Lead Contacted** → Sales rep reaches out
5. **Lead Qualified** → BANT criteria confirmed
6. **Lead Converted** → Becomes Account, Contact, Opportunity
7. **Opportunity Worked** → Progress through stages
8. **Quote Created** → Configure products and pricing
9. **Quote Approved** → Multi-level approval
10. **Quote Accepted** → Customer accepts
11. **Contract Signed** → Deal won
12. **Invoice Sent** → Billing begins
13. **Payment Received** → Revenue recognized

### Campaign to Lead Workflow

1. **Campaign Planned** → Define objectives and budget
2. **List Built** → Create target audience
3. **Content Created** → Design email with AI
4. **Campaign Launched** → Send to audience
5. **Engagement Tracked** → Monitor opens and clicks
6. **Leads Generated** → Responders become leads
7. **Leads Routed** → Assigned to sales
8. **Follow-Up** → Sales contacts leads
9. **Conversion** → Leads become opportunities
10. **ROI Calculated** → Measure campaign success

### Case to Resolution Workflow

1. **Case Created** → From email, web, phone, chat
2. **Case Categorized** → AI categorizes automatically
3. **Case Assigned** → Routed to best agent
4. **First Response** → Agent responds (SLA)
5. **Investigation** → Diagnose issue
6. **Solution Provided** → Resolve customer issue
7. **Customer Confirms** → Customer satisfied
8. **Case Closed** → Resolution documented
9. **CSAT Survey** → Customer satisfaction measured
10. **Knowledge Updated** → Solution added to KB

### Hire to Onboard Workflow

1. **Position Created** → Manager requests headcount
2. **Position Approved** → HR and finance approve
3. **Job Posted** → Published to job boards
4. **Applications Received** → Candidates apply
5. **Candidates Screened** → AI-powered screening
6. **Interviews Conducted** → Multi-round interviews
7. **Offer Extended** → Offer letter sent
8. **Offer Accepted** → Candidate accepts
9. **Background Check** → Pre-employment screening
10. **Onboarding** → New hire integration

## Tips for Success

### Data Quality

**Keep Data Clean:**
- Complete all required fields
- Update records promptly
- Merge duplicate records
- Archive outdated records

**Maintain Consistency:**
- Use standard formats
- Follow naming conventions
- Use picklists vs. free text
- Regular data audits

### User Adoption

**Make It a Habit:**
- Start your day in HotCRM
- Log all activities immediately
- Update records in real-time
- End day with task review

**Leverage Automation:**
- Use assignment rules
- Enable workflow automation
- Set up email alerts
- Configure approval processes
