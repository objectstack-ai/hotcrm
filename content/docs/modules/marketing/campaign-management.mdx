# Campaign Management

Organize your marketing efforts and track their ROI. Connect every lead and opportunity back to the initiative that generated it.

## Features

### Multi-Channel Planning
- **Hierarchy**: Parent Campaigns (e.g., "2024 Product Launch") and Child Campaigns (e.g., "Q1 Webinar", "Email Blast").
- **Budgeting**: Track "Budgeted Cost" vs. "Actual Cost".
- **Asset Management**: Store banners, scripts, and email templates.

### Member Management
- **Targeting**: Import leads from reports or CSVs into a campaign.
- **Status Tracking**: Track member status: "Sent", "Opened", "Clicked", "Responded".

### ROI Analysis
- **Attribution**: Automatically link Opportunities to the primary Campaign Source.
- **Reporting**: Visualize "Cost per Lead" and "Cost per Won Deal".

## Common Campaign Types
1.  **Webinar**: Track registrations and attendance.
2.  **Trade Show**: Capture booth visitors.
3.  **Email Blast**: Mass communication.
4.  **PPC**: Google/Facebook ad tracking.
