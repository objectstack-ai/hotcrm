# Marketing Cloud

The **Marketing Cloud** drives demand generation through multi-channel campaigns and intelligent lead nurturing.

## Functional Capabilities

### 1. Campaign Management
Plan, execute, and track marketing initiatives.
- **Multi-Channel**: Email, Social, Events, Ads.
- **Budget Tracking**: Monitor "Actual Cost" vs "Budgeted Cost".
- **Hierarchy**: Parent/Child campaigns.

### 2. Lead Nurturing
- **Drip Campaigns**: Automated email sequences.
- **Behavioral Scoring**: Score leads based on website visits and email clicks.

### 3. ROI Analytics
- **Attribution**: Link closed revenue back to the originating campaign.
- **Cost Analysis**: Calculate Cost Per Lead (CPL) and ROI.

## Integration Points

- **Sales Cloud**: Hands off "Marketing Qualified Leads" (MQL) to sales.
