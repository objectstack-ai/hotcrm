### Account Health Score

HotCRM automatically calculates a health score (0-100) to identify at-risk accounts:

**Factors Considered:**
- **Engagement** (40 points) - Recent meetings, calls, email frequency
- **Financial** (30 points) - On-time payments, upsell activity, revenue trend
- **Relationship** (30 points) - Executive sponsor, multiple contacts engaged

**Use Cases:**
- **80-100**: Upsell opportunity - Engage for expansion
- **60-79**: Healthy - Maintain regular cadence
- **40-59**: At risk - Schedule executive review
- **0-39**: Critical - Immediate intervention required

## Opportunity Management (Pipeline)

### What is an Opportunity?

An Opportunity represents a specific sales dealâ€”a potential or actual sale to an account. This is where you track deal progress, forecast revenue, and manage your sales pipeline.

---

### The 7-Stage Sales Process

Every opportunity progresses through these stages:

#### Stage 1: Prospecting (10% Win Probability)
**What's Happening:** Initial contact made, interest confirmed, basic fit established

**Exit Criteria:** Meeting scheduled, budget discussed, timeline identified

**Duration:** 1-2 weeks

---

#### Stage 2: Qualification (20% Win Probability)
**What's Happening:** BANT qualification (Budget, Authority, Need, Timeline)

**BANT Framework:**
- âœ… **Budget**: Can they afford it?
- âœ… **Authority**: Who approves?
- âœ… **Need**: Why do they need it?
- âœ… **Timeline**: When will they buy?

**Duration:** 1-2 weeks

---

#### Stage 3: Needs Analysis (40% Win Probability)
**What's Happening:** Deep discovery, demo/POC, solution design

**Exit Criteria:** Requirements documented, demo successful, technical validation passed

**Duration:** 2-4 weeks

---

#### Stage 4: Proposal (60% Win Probability)
**What's Happening:** Formal proposal submitted, pricing presented

**Exit Criteria:** Proposal reviewed by stakeholders, questions answered

**Duration:** 1-2 weeks

---

#### Stage 5: Negotiation (80% Win Probability)
**What's Happening:** Contract negotiations, pricing discussions, implementation planning

**Exit Criteria:** Terms agreed, pricing finalized, legal review complete

**Duration:** 1-3 weeks

---

#### Stage 6: Closed Won (100% - Victory! ğŸ‰)
**Deal Characteristics:** Contract signed, purchase order received

**Next Steps:** Handoff to customer success, schedule implementation

---

#### Stage 7: Closed Lost (0% - Learn and Move On)
**Common Reasons:** Lost to competitor, no budget, no decision/timing

**Important Actions:** Document loss reason, request feedback, stay in touch
